## Claim Discipline

The benchmark record above supports these public claims:

- RWIF retrieval is functioning and measurable on the shipped micro-benchmark.
- RWIF wave retrieval stays in agreement with dense cosine on a shipped real text-derived array dataset.
- RWIF wave retrieval can preserve top-1 agreement with dense cosine on a larger shipped text corpus even when deeper rankings diverge.
- RWIF semantic memory can answer grounded questions with provenance-backed memory objects.
- The larger semantic verifier path has been exercised with replay and soak measurements.

The benchmark record above does not yet support stronger public claims such as universal superiority over vector databases, storage-frontier wins across arbitrary corpora, or broad hardware-level latency guarantees. Those claims require dedicated comparative benchmarking and should be added only after reproducible measurements are published.
